Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pickles do you offer? ▼
We offer a variety of pickles including mango, lemon, garlic, and mixed vegetable pickles.
How can I place an order? ▼
You can place an order by visiting the Order page and selecting your desired pickles.
What is the shelf life of your pickles? ▼
Our pickles have a shelf life of up to 6 months when stored in a cool, dry place.
Do you offer international shipping? ▼
Currently, we only offer shipping within the country.
Can I return a product? ▼
Yes, you can return a product within 14 days of purchase if it's unopened and in its original packaging.
Are your pickles homemade? ▼
Yes, all our pickles are made in small batches at home using traditional recipes.
Do you use good quality ingredients? ▼
Absolutely! We source only the freshest and highest quality ingredients for our pickles.
Where do you get your ingredients? ▼
Our ingredients are sourced from local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure quality and freshness.
